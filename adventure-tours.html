Mountain Bike Adventure Tours

When was the last time you went on an outdoor adventure that tested your physical endurance and offered you breathtaking views? Our bike tours are one-of-a-kind and completely customizable to your needs. All skill levels welcome!

Bitty Bikers - Triple Tree Trail - Daily Rides*

This mountain biking adventure is geared for children ages 4-17. This is a 5-mile adventure tour on moderate terrain. Child must be able to ride a bike long distances without training wheels and may be required to demonstrate ability before heading out on the trail. Children under 10 must be accompanied by a parent/guardian. We provide 1 adventure guide per every 10 registered children.

Price: $10 per child
Bitty Bikers Annual Membership: $120 per child

Terms: *Rides run Monday-Saturday at 8 am, 10am, 12pm, and 2pm
Tours canceled in inclement weather.
Pre-Registration required 48-hrs in advance. Cancellation 24-hrs prior.

Customize Your Own Adventure

This mountain biking adventure is for intermediate riders who want to explore the Bozeman area. You'll work with our trail experts to find the trail that's right for you. Tour includes one professional trail guide. Stop by the store or contact us for more information. Price is per person per mile.

Don't want an experienced adventure guide? We'll help you plan your route for FREE*.
*in-store offer only

Price per person/per mile: $8

Terms: Rides run Monday-Saturday at 7 am, 10am, 1pm, and 3pm
Tours canceled in inclement weather; light rains okay
Pre-Registration required 48-hrs in advance. Cancellation 48-hrs prior.

2-Day 1-Night Biker Bootcamp - Bridger Foothills Trail

This 22.7 mile black diamond mountain biking adventure is for intense riders who want to explore Bozeman on a deeper level. You'll be accompanied by two "Bootcamp Trainers" for an overnight campout at the halfway point. Bootcamp Trainers are expert riders who are well-versed in wilderness-survival, first-aid, and the Bozeman area.

Price: $150 per person

Terms: Rides run Monday-Saturday at 7 am, 10am, 1pm, and 3pm
Minimum of 5 riders required per Bootcamp session. Adventure will be postponed if number of registrants falls below this number.
Includes: 2 Meals-Ready-to-Eat (1 dinner; 1 breakfast). Riders are responsible for bringing their own gear and other overnight essentials, including snacks.
