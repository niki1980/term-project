Bikers come from around the world to attend "Cycle the Continental Divide," an annual event hosted by The Mountain Spoke. We also host "Girls Bike it Better" and Bozeman Beginning Bikers for local riders.

Cycle the Continental Divide

August 6-16, 2018

$1000 per rider

This ride takes place on the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route, one of the most recognized and important off-pavement cycling routes in the United States, if not the world! The route crisscrosses the Continental Divide from north to south starting in Banff, Alberta, Canada and finishing at the US/Mexico border in Antelope Wells, New Mexico. Our event covers the section of the trail from Helena, MT to Colter Bay, WY. This portion includes 449.6 miles of breath-taking scenery. Riders are welcome to extend their rides before or after the event dates to experience more of what this great road has to offer.

Terms:
2 Trail Guides per event
Designated pit-stops with 10 nights lodging
Meals are on own and riders.
Bikes and equipment available for a fee.

Girls Bike it Better - Mystic Lake Trail

September 1, 2018

$100 per rider

Kick-off Labor day weekend with our 5th Annual - "Girls Bike it Better" event! Ladies come from far and wide to hit the trails in beautiful Bozeman. This year, ladies will hit the Mystic Lake trailhead for 20 miles of moderate riding. Individuals and groups are welcome. If you are interested in a private ladies-only event, speak to one of our associates - they'll be happy to customize a tour for you and accommodate your interests.
