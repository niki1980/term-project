The Mountain Spoke is a retail store located in beautiful Bozeman, Montana. We specialize in bikes, bike parts, bike repair, bike tours, and special events. We sponsor and organize custom tours throughout the season and promote several events including "Cycle the Continental Divide," a ride that draws enthusiasts from around the world. Whether you're just getting into the sport or are an avid biker, our team of passionate experts is here to assist you with all of your biking needs.
